## Role Overview

Morgan Stanley is seeking a **Data Engineer (Python)** to join the **Institutional Securities Technology** team, supporting **Prime Brokerage** and **Institutional Equity** platforms. The role focuses on modernizing client reporting systems, working closely with business users, and delivering high-visibility data and reporting solutions.

This is an **Associate-level** position responsible for developing and maintaining software solutions that directly support business needs while gaining exposure to a high-growth, industry-leading financial services environment.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Collaborate with business users to gather requirements, analyze them in detail, and write clear specifications
- Translate business requirements into technical designs
- Implement data and reporting solutions
- Enhance existing applications to meet new requirements
- Address ad-hoc reporting requests using existing tools or by implementing new solutions
- Investigate, resolve, and provide feedback on data-quality issues
- Provide **L3 production support**
